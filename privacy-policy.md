# Privacy Policy for HiNikki


## Introduction

HiNikki ("we", "our", or "us") operates the HiNikki mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with your use of the App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

By using HiNikki, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

## Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the App:

- Names, relationships and notes about the older adult and their family
- Family photos uploaded by caregivers
- Home and familiar addresses, and safe-place locations
- Precise device location of the older adult (foreground, shared with linked family)
- Messages exchanged with the Nikki assistant
- Account email and password for caregiver/admin accounts

This information is used solely to provide and improve the App's functionality. We do not sell, rent, or share this information with third parties except as described in this policy.

## Device Permissions

The App requests the following device permissions to function correctly:

- **location-when-in-use**: used to provide core App functionality
- **photo-library**: used to provide core App functionality

Permission access is used only for the stated purpose and is not used to identify or track you.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any external services. No data is transmitted to third-party servers.

## Data Retention

We retain collected information only for as long as necessary to provide the App's functionality. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us (see Contact section below). Upon verified request, we will delete your personal data within 30 days.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove such information from our systems.
